Frog

Frogs have roamed the Earth for more than 200 million years, at least as long as the dinosaurs.

  • Scientific Name: Anura
  • Average Length: 7 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Riversides

A frog is a member of a diverse and largely carnivorous group of short-bodied, tailless amphibians. Frogs are widely distributed, ranging from the tropics to subarctic regions, but the most diversity can be found in tropical rainforest.
